## article: 对话：做题家的修行病精英主义VS人人本自具足.md
**target**: karma-diagnosis
**gap**: Even positive links (e.g., linking with nature) can paradoxically activate arrogance and loneliness -- the person feels "only I can appreciate this beauty," reinforcing elitism and isolation. The skill discusses elitism broadly but not this specific mechanism where a healthy link gets co-opted by ego.
**suggestion**: Add a pattern in karma-diagnosis: "链接反被小我劫持 -- 接触大自然等正面链接时反而激活傲慢心和孤独感，认为只有自己能欣赏，这暴露了区隔心业障" (when linking with nature triggers arrogance/loneliness, the link is being co-opted by elitist ego). Also note the diagnostic question about whether a 农村老奶奶 living peacefully counts as enlightened -- this tests the user's elitist framing of enlightenment.
