**target**: alienation
**gap**: The alienation skill covers four-fold labor alienation but doesn't address consumption/consumerism as a distinct dimension of alienation — Marx's specific argument that alienated labor drives people to fill the void with consumption, creating a cycle where "每个人都力图创造出一种支配他人的异己的本质力量" through creating new needs in others.
